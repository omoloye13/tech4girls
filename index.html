About Tech4girls Initiatives

Igniting an Empowered Womanhood

Tech4girls Initiatives (T4GI) is a Nigerian non-governmental, not for profit girls/women development platform to intervene in the socialization and realization of a society where women are visible and are treated equally as men.Technology is a billion dollar industry that is widely used to solve various problem but it is unfortunate that we find it difficult to see women there as it is dominated by men. The major challenges women face in the tech space is that they believe they don't have the right education to fit into the field, irrespective of the course you studied or what you want to study technology is used to solve different type of problems be it education, health, research or even product management. With technology, you could make yourself self employed or you could employ people and help the world a particular problem we need more women to join the tech world because there are lots of challenges which i believe it is only women that can solve them let me welcome you all as you join the technological world.

Training women/girls in digital skills

T4GI is women fueling Technology, a digital advocacy campaign and media project for girls and women to explore the roles of technology in advancing gender equality it also highlights the gender gap in technology and advocates for women and girls access to and control of technology. At T4GI we are set to train women and girls in digital skills, soft skills to bridge the gap of low self confidence amongst women in our society and also to prepare the girl child empowered as they gradually move into womanhood, at the end of the training we will be organising an hackathon where the technological skills they have gotten will be used to solve real life solutions.We do not focus on empowering the girl child or women alone, we also advocate for feminist ideology, protection of the young females and women, adolescent sexuality, gender & human right issues and equal opportunities for all.

Donations

In Nigeria, women and girls face incredible challenges to their safety, success, human rights and equality. Your donations are essential to help us reach more women and girls with our training programs to increase the number of women working in Tech, our awareness programs for young girls to maintain a proper menstrual hygiene/distribution of sanitary pads and awareness of their rights for gender equality. Your donation of any amount will help T4GI to operate in more states and to carry out its pillars of work: Advocacy and Training, Technology Capacity Building and Protection of girls and women. Together, we can make sure that there are more women and girls around the world who have real and equal opportunities, and who learn about their human rights, in Technological education and employment. Your support will help T4GI in building a bridge between technology and gender equality, while building a network to exchange knowledge and resources among regions.
