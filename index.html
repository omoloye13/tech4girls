CREATING A WORLD OF WOMEN INNOVATION AND EQUALITY

Igniting an Empowered Womanhood

Tech4girls Initiatives (T4GI) is a Nigerian non-governmental, not for profit girls/women development platform to intervene in the socialization and realization of a society where women are visible and are treated equally as men. T4GI is women fueling Technology, a digital advocacy campaign and media project for girls and women to explore the roles of technology in advancing gender equality it also highlights the gender gap in technology and advocates for women and girls access to and control of technology.
